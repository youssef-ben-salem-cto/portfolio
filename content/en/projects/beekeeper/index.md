<h2 class="text-3xl font-bold text-text-primary mb-2">Beekeeper Tech Implementation</h2>
<p class="text-text-secondary text-lg">Next-generation Agritech platform powering digital apiaries and connected hives.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="prose prose-invert max-w-none text-text-secondary space-y-4">
<p>The Beekeeper Tech platform is a masterclass in modern application design, uniting mobile convenience with enterprise-scale backend processing. Built on <strong>Clean Code</strong> and strict <strong>Clean Architecture</strong> principles, this platform provides agritech professionals with real-time analytics and management capabilities for their apiaries.</p>
<p>The mobile application, built entirely with <strong>Flutter</strong>, provides a seamless, high-performance experience across both <strong>iOS & Android</strong> platforms. The backend infrastructure is a sophisticated <strong>Microservices</strong> architecture powered by <strong>Nest JS</strong> and <strong>LoopBack JS</strong>, orchestrated to handle high-frequency data from connected IoT hive devices.</p>
</div>
